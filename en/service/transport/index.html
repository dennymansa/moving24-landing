Honest about pricing

What's included in the price, and what costs extra.

Nobody likes surprises on the invoice. Every move is different: floor, lift, volume, parking. That's why there's no fixed price list. We calculate your specific job and tell you the price upfront, with no hidden fees.

Always included in the price

  • Movers and a driver
  • Van and fuel within Tallinn
  • Moving blankets, straps and dollies
  • Loading and unloading
  • Initial assessment and quote

Charged separately

  • Packing materials (boxes, wrap)
  • Full packing service
  • Furniture disassembly and assembly
  • Extra-heavy items (piano, safe)
  • Junk removal and disposal
  • Long-distance routes beyond Harju County
