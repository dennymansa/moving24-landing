Story

Who we are

Moving24 OU is a moving and transport company based in Tallinn. Registry code 16978239, VAT number EE102734342, address Tartu mnt 2, 10145 Tallinn. These are not just words on a page, they are entries in the Estonian business registry that you can verify yourself. So you know exactly who you are dealing with before the first box is even lifted.

We have handled more than 5,000 moves: apartments, houses, offices, warehouses, and all the things other movers turn down. A piano from the third floor, a safe from the cellar, a single parcel or a hundred boxes - to us these are all jobs that can be done properly. That is exactly why people come to us after they have heard no everywhere else. We work 24/7 and serve Tallinn, Harju County, and all of Estonia, including evenings and weekends.

Our mission is simple: to make every move a positive, easy, and stress-free experience, for individuals and businesses alike. We quote the price upfront and honestly, we handle your belongings with care, and we keep the whole process calm from start to finish. Your things arrive intact, on time, and at the price we agreed on.
