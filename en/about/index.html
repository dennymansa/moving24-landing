Story

Who we are

Moving24 is a Tallinn moving company people call when they just need it done right. A piano from the third floor, an office over the weekend, one load or a hundred boxes. We've handled more than 5,000 moves, and we know every box is somebody's home.

Our job is to keep your day calm. We quote the price up front and honestly, handle your things with care and stick to the time we agreed. You stay relaxed, we take care of the rest.
